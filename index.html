The Benefits Of Attending Business Conferences

In the digital age, many wonder about the relevance of business conferences. Now we can all benefit from a rich variety of resources to hone our professional skills without ever leaving the comfort of our office or home. With the advent of social media, webinars, blogs, and replayed conferences, are business conferences a thing of the past? Absolutely not!

However, you have to attend conferences where you can easily justify the expense and time invested. A business conference well suited to your needs can be a real springboard for your career for several reasons.

Build Your Professional Network

In history, some of the best remedies, inventions and discoveries have been made by great minds - united in the quest for knowledge - who have come together to collaborate better. Generations X and Y therefore also have everything to gain by continuing this old tradition.


Attending a conference related to your field of interest is the perfect opportunity to meet other professionals who share similar needs and challenges to increase your opportunities for collaboration. Get out of your comfort zone and enjoy! 

Dealing With Trade-shows

More often than not, people are reluctant to visit showrooms for fear of having to talk to sellers for too long. Yet some of these salespeople are the best people to know to take the pulse of an industry's current business climate, new products and services to stay competitive, and significant advances from direct and indirect competitors. Make it your goal to meet some of them and try to make them your allies!

Listen To Great Speakers

One of the main benefits of attending a conference is when you get to hear a great keynote speaker give their presentation. “Generally speaking, the keynote speaker is a top expert in their field who synthesizes their expertise into a brief speech that allows you to learn a great deal from them in a short amount of time” says Emily Miller, the managing director at Keynote Speaker.


Some event have motivational speakers who can fire you up and tell great stories that motivate you. “Unlike most guest speakers, an inspirational speaker can share stories that will compel you in a powerful way” says Steve Adams, one of the event managers at Motivational Speakers. “When you are able to hear a great speech that inspires you, it can literally alter the direction of your entire life. Just ask Marc Benioff who started SalesForce after hearing a Tony Robbins speech.”

Engage the Attendees

Talk to the participants, take an interest in their needs, their knowledge, their challenges and their questions. Don't just talk about yourself. By listening more, you will be able to know how you could possibly work closely together or even make them your future customers.


When a potential business connection is clearly established, take the opportunity to promote your skills and what you could do for them. But remember, it happens gradually!


There is no point in starting a sales pitch without knowing that the other party is inclined to listen and take advantage of it. Also listen to people's opinions on your offer and take their suggestions and recommendations into consideration in order to improve it accordingly.

International Business Conferences

International business conferences bring together people from different geographic regions working in the same discipline or field. They are therefore a great way to develop new business relationships with people you would not have met otherwise. Ask them questions, learn more about how people in your profession work elsewhere in the world. Perhaps you could improve your processes, or help them improve theirs, and thus broaden your network of influence?


Attending a business conference also has other advantages and opportunities such as finding a new job, or recruiting qualified people to fill an important position without having to deal with an expensive recruitment firm.

Stay Current

In the digital age, technologies and knowledge evolve at a breakneck pace and it becomes increasingly difficult and laborious to stay abreast of new developments.


Fortunately, the job of good event planners is to orchestrate informative and inspiring business conferences. To do this, the event coordinators conduct detailed research before choosing the speakers for any event. A well-thought-out event can therefore be the golden opportunity to introduce you to the novelties of your favorite field.


This is a condensed overview of what should grab your attention so you can better evolve accordingly. By attending, you will be able to learn more about the advances in your field by some famous personalities. Plus, you could benefit from a refreshing vision and solutions brought to you by newer players in your industry.


Unlike anything done online, attending a conference allows you to share in person the work and research of your peers and quickly benefit from their advice to improve your own practice.


Does the event you attend offer workshops that require active participation? Once again, step out of your comfort zone and be part of it! Much research has shown that information retention is greatly increased by direct application of new knowledge, versus simple passive listening.

Get Motivated

In the heat of the moment, we too often tell ourselves that we will eventually review and improve our ways of doing things in order to reach or exceed our goals. Unfortunately, over time, we get stuck in our routine and gradually lose our motivation. According to MotivationPing.com, one of the most common reasons why people lose motivation is because they become stagnant and this is where attending a conference can come into play.


Attending a conference is the opportunity to learn and challenge our status quo. These few days spent away from your usual work environment can prove to be the long-awaited break, even the necessary breath to meditate on your business habits with a new perspective.


Frequently, participants come out of a good conference with renewed motivation. They feel better equipped to meet challenges and pitfalls. Think about the feeling of renewal that comes with every January 1st . We reflect on past events and set goals to become better versions of ourselves for the year to come. Then we put them into action. Take advantage of business conferences to immerse yourself in this same state of mind. You will come out stronger!

Final Thoughts

Although we have highlighted the importance of business conferences, we must not forget that they require a minimum of preparation. Do your research beforehand and make sure the event you want to attend matches your real needs. Consult the program carefully prepared by the " event planner " in question and choose the conferences and workshops that will benefit you.


Also take a look at the list of exhibitors present. Finally, set yourself goals to achieve when participating. These will help you get a better return on your investment!
